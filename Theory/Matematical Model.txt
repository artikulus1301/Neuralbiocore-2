Sleep/Wake Model:
State = {Wake, N1, N2, N3 (SWS), REM}

Transitions:
Wake -> N1 -> N2 -> N3 -> REM -> N2 -> ... (90 min cycles)

During SWS (Slow Wave Sleep):

- Frequency: delta-waves (0.5-4 Hz), omega_i -> 1-2 Hz
- Activity: A_i down_down, R_sync -> high (hypersynchronization)
- Function: Consolidation of declarative memory
- Process:
  
  for epoch in SWS:
      Reactivation of hippocampal patterns:
      X_replay ~ sharp-wave ripples (150-250 Hz)
      
      Transfer to neocortex:
      w_cortex += eta * < X_hippocampus(t-delta) * X_cortex(t) >
      
      Synaptic downscaling:
      w_ij -> w_ij * (1 - delta_SWS * [1 - importance_ij])

During REM:

- Frequency: theta (4-8 Hz), beta (15-30 Hz), gamma
- Activity: A_i high, but D(t) = 0 (sensory input disconnected)
- Function: Training generative model, procedural memory, forgetting
- Process:
  
  for epoch in REM:
      Generation of "dreams":
      X_dream(t) ~ p(X | w, H_random, a_random)
      
      Training predictions:
      Minimize: F_generative = -log p(X_dream | w)
      
      Erasing weak connections:
      w_ij -> w_ij - eta_forget * (1 - importance_ij)
      
      Consolidation of motor skills:
      w_motor += eta_REM * < epsilon_action * d_mu/d_w >

Sleep Deprivation:

if sleep_debt > critical:
    -> theta_M increases (reduced plasticity)
    -> w_ij unregularized (overfitting)
    -> Pi(t) increases (anxiety)
    -> Phi(X) decreases (microsleeps, fragmentation of consciousness)

17. GABAergic Modulation and Anesthesia

17a. Inhibitory Dynamics

GABA Receptors (GABA_A and GABA_B):

I_GABA_i(t) = g_GABA_A * (V_i - E_Cl) * s_GABA_A(t) 
            + g_GABA_B * (V_i - E_K) * s_GABA_B(t)

Notation:
- g_GABA_A/B: Conductance of GABA receptors
- E_Cl ~ -70 mV, E_K ~ -90 mV (reversal potentials)
- s_GABA(t): Synaptic variable

GABA Synaptic Dynamics:

ds_GABA_A/dt = alpha_A * [GABA](t) * (1 - s_GABA_A) - beta_A * s_GABA_A
ds_GABA_B/dt = alpha_B * [GABA](t) * (1 - s_GABA_B) - beta_B * s_GABA_B

Time Constants:
- GABA_A: Fast (tau ~ 10-20 ms) — phasic inhibition
- GABA_B: Slow (tau ~ 100-300 ms) — tonic inhibition

17b. Updated Neuron Equation:

tau_i * dV_i/dt = -V_i(t) 
                + R_i * [Sum_j w_ij^exc * s_j^exc(t) - Sum_k w_ik^inh * s_k^inh(t) + u_i(t)]
                - I_GABA_i(t)
                + h_i(t) 
                + sigma_i * xi_i(t)

17c. Interneurons (PV, SST, VIP)

Three classes of inhibitory interneurons:

1. Parvalbumin+ (PV) — Fast, perisomatic:
tau_PV * dV_PV/dt = -V_PV + Sum_j w_PV,j * x_j(t)

x_PV(t) = f_fast(V_PV - theta_PV)  # high-frequency spikes

Targets: Soma of pyramidal neurons
Function: winner-take-all, gamma rhythms

2. Somatostatin+ (SST) — Dendritic:
tau_SST * dV_SST/dt = -V_SST + Sum_j w_SST,j * x_j(t) + top_down(X_{l+1})

x_SST(t) = f_slow(V_SST - theta_SST)

Targets: Apical dendrites
Function: Modulation of top-down signals

3. Vasoactive Intestinal Peptide+ (VIP) — Disinhibition:
VIP -> SST -|  (Inhibits SST)
VIP -> PV -|

Result: Disinhibition
Function: Signal amplification, attention

Microcircuit:

Excitatory (E) -> PV -> E  (feed-forward inhibition)
E -> SST -> E_dendrites     (modulatory inhibition)
E -> VIP -> SST -> E         (disinhibition)

17d. Anesthesia: Mechanism of Action

Propofol, Isoflurane, Sevoflurane:

Potentiate GABA_A receptors:

g_GABA_A^anes(t) = g_GABA_A * [1 + k_anes * [Anesthetic](t)]

Where k_propofol ~ 5-10

Effect on System:

1. Increase in tonic inhibition: V_i -> more hyperpolarized state
2. Decrease in excitability: x_i(t) down_down
3. Disruption of thalamo-cortical loops: Thalamus <-> Cortex connection weakens
4. Destruction of phase synchronization: R(t) -> 0
5. Fragmentation of Global Workspace: A_i(t) < theta_consciousness for all i
6. Collapse of Integration: Phi(X) -> 0

Equation under Anesthesia:

dX_l/dt = -X_l/tau_l + F_l(...) - K_anes * [Anes](t) * X_l

Where K_anes -> infinity at deep anesthesia

Anesthesia Stages:

[Anes]    Effect                           Phi(X)    R_sync
------------------------------------------------------------------------
0-1 uM    Sedation, drowsiness              0.4       0.6
1-3 uM    Loss of consciousness, amnesia    0.1       0.3
3-5 uM    Surgical anesthesia               0.01      0.1
>5 uM     Suppression of brainstem reflexes ~0        ~0

Emergence:

d[Anes]/dt = -lambda_clearance * [Anes](t)

As clearance occurs:
1. Thalamus reactivates
2. R_sync recovers (alpha -> beta -> gamma rhythms)
3. A_i exceeds threshold -> consciousness returns
4. Phi(X) grows

Hysteresis:
Falling asleep != Waking up (different thresholds due to receptor adaptation):
[Anes]_sleep > [Anes]_wake

18b. Two-Dimensional Model of Affect (Russell's Circumplex)

Valence:

dV/dt = (1/tau_V) * [mu_V - V(t)] + beta_reward * delta_RPE(t) + beta_pain * Pain(t)

where:
mu_V(t) = f_vmPFC(X_context(t), Goals(t))  — Baseline tone

19. Theory of Mind — Second-Order Recursion

19a. Basic Structure

Mentalization Levels:

Level 0: Self-model
  M_0(t) = r(X(t), a(t), Theta(t))
  
Level 1: Other-model
  M_1_other(t) = r_ToM(O_observable(t), Context(t))
  
Level 2: Self-modeling-other-modeling-self
  M_2_self(t) = "What do they think of me?"
  M_2_self(t) = r_ToM(M_1_other(t), My_actions(t))

Notation:
- M_0 — My model of self
- M_1_other — My model of another person
- M_2 — My model of what the other thinks of me
- r_ToM — Mentalistic inference function

19b. Mathematical Formalization

Model of Other as Generative Model:

p(O_behavior | M_other) = Integral p(O | a, X_other) * p(a | Goals_other, X_other) 
                            * p(X_other | M_other) dX_other da

Inverse Inference:

p(M_other | O_behavior) proportional to p(O_behavior | M_other) * p(M_other)

i.e., observing behavior O, we infer mental state

19c. Components of ToM

1. Beliefs:

B_other(t) = {what they know, what they believe}

dB_other/dt = f_update(O_other_observable(t), History)

Example: False Belief Task (Sally-Anne)
  B_self: Ball is in basket B
  B_Sally: Ball is in basket A (she didn't see the move)

2. Desires/Goals:

G_other(t) = arg max_{g} p(a_other | g, B_other)

19e. Mathematical Model of Recursion

Level 0: Self-model

19f. Recursive Update Equations

General form:

M_{n+1}(t) = r_ToM(M_n(t), O_level_n(t), Context(t))

Minimization via:
dM_n/dt = -dF_ToM/dM_n

Interpretation:
